cricnews App Help Page

Welcome to the cricnews App Help Page. Here you can find information on how to use our services and answers to common questions.

Personal Data Shared with us by cricnews App/Website:

To use the cricnews App/Website services or to Register / Login, you have to provide the following information:
A. Mobile Number: We will validate and verify your mobile number via an OTP process.
(i) Mobile number is required to handle your queries.
(ii) Sometimes, the mobile number can be used to send you custom offers related information.
If you don’t want to share your mobile number, we will not be able to provide our App services to you.
B. Email address: cricnews user email address will be used for service notifications (User activity on cricnews App & Service related notifications). Other use cases include:
(i) We will verify your email address to detect fraud/unauthorized usage or access to the cricnews App.
(ii) We will use your email address to send you latest government job alerts, payout/rewards related notifications, and to respond to queries related to the use of cricnews App services.
When you register for cricnews App, we may further reach out to update your profile to provide better custom-based latest government job alerts/offers that are more relevant to you. Once you agree to our privacy policy, you give us consent to use the information provided by you. If you are not comfortable with providing the information, please do not use cricnews app/website services.

Use of Personal Data We Capture:

The information is captured from your mobile phone after you give permission to the cricnews App. This information can be taken from third parties in certain cases. This data is used to provide the best services to our users and to improve the overall experience or to check for fraud or unauthorized activities. All information is captured after user permission wherever required.
(i) Mobile Device Information – We capture hardware, device model, manufacture details, unique device identifiers, and network information along with the phone number. We use this information to detect fraud and unauthorized installs and to monitor your activities on the cricnews App.
(ii) Log Data – When you use cricnews services, we collect some data logs. These may include:

  • Reading SMS messages for OTP verification.
  • How you use our App.
  • IP Address.
  • Crashes, system activity, hardware settings, the date and time of your request, and referral URL.
  • Cookies.
(iii) Location Data – While using the cricnews App, we may collect and store your location using IP address, GPS, or any other mobile hardware. This information will be used to sustain and improve user experience.
(iv) Unique Application Numbers – This can be used to get information about your services install or uninstall via the cricnews App. This will help us in keeping track of cashback offers used by the cricnews App and to provide better services within the App.
(v) Native Storage – We may use/store some application data or caches in native storage. This information is used to maintain login sessions and to sync with cricnews servers and update services we deliver via the cricnews App for future use.
(vi) Cookies – We may use cookies or similar technology to classify your device. We may use this information to communicate with services we offer to our partners. Such information can appear on other websites or SDKs we use for analytics that will help us in Traffic or Crash analytics.

Disclosure/Share of Personal Data:

Data collected by us is not shared with any organization, company, or individual outside cricnews except for the following conditions:
(i) With your Approval – We share device-level information with our partners when you provide approval for the same. When you agree to our privacy policy, you provide us with the consent to share the same. This information will be shared with 3rd party service providers to personalize your App experience and for analytics purposes.
(ii) Outside Processing – This can be for analytical processing or for any legal reasons.

Children’s Privacy:

cricnews app services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can do the necessary actions.

Friend Invites/Referral:

If you choose to use any of our Invites/Referral services for informing friends about our site/mobile app, you will be asked to provide your friends' names, mobile numbers, and e-mail addresses. You may enter this information manually, or you can also choose to import your contacts from your mobile device, email address book, or Facebook address book in which case you will be asked to provide us with your email address, which will be used solely for the purpose of importing your contacts within the referral service. We will then automatically send your friends a one-time text message, WhatsApp message, or e-mail, plus a reminder (both of which may include your name, mobile number, and email address) inviting them to visit our site/mobile app. We ask for this information for the sole purpose of sending these emails and tracking the success of our referral program.

Survey Serving Technology:

This app uses Pollfish SDK. Pollfish is an online survey platform through which anyone may conduct surveys. Pollfish collaborates with Developers of applications for smartphones in order to have access to users of such applications and address survey questionnaires to them. When a user connects to this app, a specific set of user’s device data (including Advertising ID which may be processed by Pollfish only in strict compliance with Google Play policies- and/or other device data) and response metadata is automatically sent to Pollfish servers, in order for Pollfish to discern whether the user is eligible for a survey. For a full list of data received by Pollfish through this app, please read carefully Pollfish respondent terms located at Pollfish respondent terms. These data will be associated with your answers to the questionnaires whenever Pollfish sends such questionnaires to eligible users. By downloading the application, you accept this privacy policy document and you hereby give your consent for the processing by Pollfish of the aforementioned data. Furthermore, you are informed that you may disable Pollfish operation at any time by using the Pollfish “opt out section” available on Pollfish's website. We once more invite you to check the respondent’s terms of use if you wish to have a more detailed view of the way Pollfish works.
